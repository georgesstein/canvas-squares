• To create a square double click* on a free space on the canvas
  • To select a square left click on the square
  • To unselect a square left click on a free space on the canvas or select another square
  • To resize a square select a square, hover over the square and use the mouse wheel
  • To enable a socket select square and left click on socket
  • To drag and drop a square left click on the square, hold, move and release the mouse button
  • To delete a square right click on the square
  • To delete a socket right click on the socket of the selected square
  • To draw an arrow connection unselect square (if selected), left click on start socket and left click on end socket
  • To delete an arrow connection delete socket (start or end)
* this application is currently adapted for mouse control.
